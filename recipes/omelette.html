Description

A true French omelette is just eggs and butter, no filling. The egg is folded for a soft, tender texture. The technique does take a bit of practice to perfect but you'll be amazed at just how delicious these few ingredients can be when elevated using this method

The Allrecipes community adores this omelette recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • cheese
  • salt
  • love

Steps

  1. Break and whisk the eggs
  2. Add the cheese
  3. Fry it in the pan
  4. Enjoy it
